Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipes

Homemade Chocolate Chip cookies

This homemade chocolate chip cookie recipe will blow your mind. The perfect combination of crunchy outside with gooey inside. The aroma of these cookies baking will bring everyone within a 10 mile radius wanting to know when the next batch is coming.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Chips/Bar (I prefer one part dark chocolate one part milk chocolate)
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 tsp of Cinnamon
  • 2 stick of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Brown butter in cooking pan, this gives a toasty flavor to the cookies
  2. In a bowl mix your eggs, vanilla, sugars, cinammon, and melted butter
  3. In a seperate bowl add your flour, baking soda, and salt and mix
  4. Mix both the wet and dry ingredients together
  5. Cut your chocolate bar into chunks, then pour the chunks and the chocolate chips into the mixture
  6. Optional: Sit in the fridge overnight so the flavors can meet each other
  7. Bake in oven at 350 for 10-15 minutes depending on how gooey you like your cookies
  8. When done baking, let the cookies sit on the counter for 10-20 minutes
  9. Grab a glass of milk and enjoy!
